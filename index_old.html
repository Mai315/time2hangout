What do we do?

We're not "just a garage".
We're a place to hangout.
At The Hangout, we offer clubhouse amenities to all riders, including bike storage, tools, lockers, bike wash, shower & laundry facilities. When we are not riding, We also do adventure tours, scuba diving, skydiving etc.

Adventure Trails

Dirt bike, 4x4, hiking, camping. We are into all things adventure. We often organize trips to various waterfalls to enjoy some cooling times.

Overseas Trips

Check out our last trip to himalayan. The scenery is amazing. Soak in the icey water if you dare, just like the "Three idiots".

Diving and Liveaboard!

We dive at various south east asia locations, such as Tioman, Raja Ampat Islands, The Gili Islands and Batam. We organize trips to further locations such as Maldives as well. Look out for our event page.
