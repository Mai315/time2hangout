What do we do?

We're not "just a garage".
We're a place to hangout.
At Team Hangout, we offer clubhouse amenities to all riders, including bike rental, bike storage, tools, lockers, shower, and laundry.
When we are not riding, we also do adventure tours, scuba diving, skydiving, and more.

Junior Program

Our Junior Program is designed for children aged five and above. Kids learn to ride with confidence while focusing on balance, coordination, throttle control, and safety—all in a controlled, supervised environment led by certified coaches.

Learn About Junior Sessions

Adventure Trails

Dirt bike, 4x4, hiking, camping—we are into all things adventure. We often organize trips to waterfalls to enjoy cooling breaks between rides.

Overseas Trips

Check out our last trip to the Himalayas. The scenery is amazing—soak in the icy water if you dare, just like the "Three Idiots".

Diving and Liveaboard

We dive across Southeast Asia—Tioman, Raja Ampat Islands, the Gili Islands, and Batam—with trips to far-off destinations like the Maldives. Keep an eye on our event page for upcoming expeditions.
